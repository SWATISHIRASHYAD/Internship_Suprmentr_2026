# *Assignment Name* : Spam Classifier Thinking
# *Description* : Design a spam detection system: features, data needed, possible mistakes.

🔹Features
Email text content
Presence of keywords (free, win, offer)
Number of links
Sender email address
Message length
🔹 Data Needed
Collection of emails labeled as Spam / Not Spam
Large dataset for better accuracy
Text preprocessing (cleaning, tokenization)
🔹 Possible Mistakes
Important emails marked as spam (False Positive)
Spam emails marked as normal (False Negative)
Overfitting on training data
Misclassification due to new/unseen words
