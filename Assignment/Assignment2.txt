*Description* : List 10 real-life AI applications you use daily and explain how ML might work behind each in 1–2 lines.

List of 10 Real-Life AI Applications
1. Google Maps

Provides real-time traffic updates and route suggestions.
➡️ ML analyzes historical traffic data and user patterns to predict fastest routes.

2. YouTube

Recommends videos based on your interests.
➡️ ML uses recommendation algorithms based on watch history and user behavior.

3. Instagram

Shows personalized feed and reels.
➡️ ML ranks posts using engagement patterns like likes, shares, and time spent.

4. Google Assistant

Responds to voice commands and queries.
➡️ ML uses speech recognition and NLP to understand and process language.

5. Amazon

Suggests products you may like.
➡️ ML uses collaborative filtering and browsing history to recommend items.

6. Netflix

Recommends movies and shows.
➡️ ML predicts preferences using viewing patterns and ratings.

7. Gmail

Filters spam emails automatically.
➡️ ML classifies emails based on patterns and previous spam data.

8. Google Photos

Automatically organizes photos and detects faces.
➡️ ML uses image recognition and clustering techniques.

9. Spotify

Creates personalized playlists.
➡️ ML analyzes listening habits and similarities between songs.

10. Phone Unlock (Face ID/Fingerprint)

Unlocks phone using face or fingerprint.
➡️ ML uses pattern recognition and deep learning for biometric matching.
